Troubleshooting

No audio

  • Press Start Audio.
  • Then press Defaults.
  • If stale, hard refresh with Ctrl+Shift+R.

MIDI repeating

  • Stop All clears held MIDI memory.
  • Held note-ons are ignored until note-off.
  • Try a drum/custom pad mode if your Launchkey is weird.

MIDI pads wrong

  • This build expects notes 36-51.
  • Readout shows MIDI note/pad fired.
  • Browser pads are unchanged.
