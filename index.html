Loop Loader + Auto Chop

Loop: a short piece of audio. Choppa cuts it into 16 playable pieces so each pad becomes one slice.
Load Loop: choose your song, beat, or sound.
Grid Chop: cuts evenly into 16 pads.
Smart Chop: tries to find drum hits.
Reverse: flips pad order for instant weird.
Start Audio → Load Loop or Demo → Hit pads. If MIDI is talking, the Reactor lights up instantly.

Performance Controls

Shape the sound: Volume is loudness, Pitch is high/low, Attack softens the start, Release changes the tail, Filter makes it darker or brighter.

FX Knob Rack

FX knobs: These are performance toys. Turn knobs to sweep, smear, crush, bend, and chop the sound while the lights react.
Knobs mirror Launchkey Mini CC 21-28. Mouse and touch controls work too, because apparently knobs must also exist on glass now.

Keyboard Sample Bank

Keyboard bank: Load one sound to play it like an instrument, or load several sounds to spread them across the keys.
Upload one sample to pitch it across the keyboard, or upload many samples to auto-map chromatically from Root Note.
