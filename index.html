X / Y

Playback

Keys 1-8 and Q-I trigger pads. Hold Loop: press and hold a pad to make it the single loop. Single Loop: tap a pad to choose the loop.

MIDI

Default: MIDI notes trigger pads. CC 20-27 control FX knobs. Tap a pad or knob, press Learn, then move a MIDI control.

Quick Start

1. Upload audio or press Demo.
2. Choose 8, 16, 32, Hits, Shuffle, Stairs, or March.
3. Trigger pads by touch, keyboard, or MIDI.
4. Use Single Loop or Hold Loop for repeating one pad.
5. Adjust FX knobs while playing.

Tools

Single Loop keeps one pad looping. Selecting another pad replaces it. Stop clears the loop.
